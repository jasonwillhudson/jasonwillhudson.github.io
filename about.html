About

My design goal is to make the interface as simple as possible, which uses the least elements but conveys the same quantity of information to the user. My design goal is to give users a clear interface and prevent their attention distracted by unnecessary elements. Programming languages I learned from school include HTML, CSS, Java, and C++.
